🧠 What is StockDuel?

StockDuel is a fast-paced trading game where users compete in real-time by choosing market direction.

Instead of traditional trading or prediction markets:

- 🟢 You join the UP side
- 🔴 Or the DOWN side
- ⚔️ And battle other players

At the end of each round:
Winners take the pool.

---

⚡ Core Experience

This is NOT a slow trading app.

StockDuel is built for:

- ⚡ Fast decisions
- 🔥 High pressure
- 🎮 Game-like interaction

Every market is a live duel.

---

⚔️ How It Works

1. Browse live markets
2. Pick a stock (NVDA, TSLA, AAPL, etc)
3. Choose your side (UP / DOWN)
4. Enter your amount
5. Wait for the round to end
6. Winners split the pool
